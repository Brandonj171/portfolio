About Me

I'm Brandon Jordan, a graduate in Resource and Technology Management with a concentration and minor in Computer Science. What started as an interest in business gradually shifted into a passion for technology, not just how things work, but how they can be built smarter, faster, and more efficiently. I’ve always been drawn to solving problems and understanding systems. The more I learned about software development, the more I enjoyed the logic, structure, and creativity behind it. Over time, coding became less about assignments and more about experimentation and improvement. I especially enjoy working with JavaScript, Python, C++, Java, SQL, React, and Node.js, and I’m constantly exploring new tools and frameworks to add to my stack. At the same time, I’ve developed a strong interest in project management, understanding how to structure a team, guide a development cycle, and deliver real outcomes. Earning my CAPM certification helped me see how much clarity and alignment matter in any tech project. I like thinking through the lifecycle: planning, execution, testing, iteration and how technical work fits into the bigger picture of product and business goals. I'm passionate about solving meaningful problems and thrive in fast-paced environments. I do my best work when I'm challenged and I am always eager to grow. Looking forward to connecting!
